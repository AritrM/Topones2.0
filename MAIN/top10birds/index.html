1.Peacock

Peacock is the most exquisite bird in the world because of its vibrant feathers and colour. The peacock is the national bird of India and national pride for all of its citizens. Peacocks mainly thrive by eating plants and small animals and are one of the largest birds in the country. You can find peacocks all over the country. They can be seen pecking the ground for food and on the tall trees in the forest. Peacocks are the males in this species of bird. The females are called peahens. 2.Swan

Swans are elegant birds known for their graceful beauty and serene nature. They are often seen gliding effortlessly across the water, their long necks arched and their white feathers shimmering in the sunlight. Swans are highly social creatures and often form strong pair bonds. They are known for their beautiful mating displays, which can include elaborate dances and vocalizations. Swans are omnivorous, feeding on a variety of plants and small animals. They play an important role in their ecosystems by helping to control plant growth and by providing food for other animals. Swans are also important cultural symbols, representing purity, love, and beauty in many cultures.
